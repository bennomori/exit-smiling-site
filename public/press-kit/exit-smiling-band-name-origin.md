# Exit Smiling Band Name Origin

## Why Exit Smiling?

Max's avid reading inspired the name Exit Smiling. He uncovered the paradox at the heart of Catch-22: trapped by rules that contradict themselves where even our escape depends on playing along with the system. Perhaps you can relate?

**Smile. Agree. Move on.**

While that might work for our world systems, it won't work for us. As a band, we have no filters, no fake exits. Just our creativity and noise telling the truth.

## Short MC / Radio Version

The name Exit Smiling was inspired by Max's reading of Catch-22 and the idea of being trapped by rules that contradict themselves. For the band, Exit Smiling means rejecting fake smiles, fake exits, and filtered versions of the truth. It is about turning pressure, contradiction, and frustration into creativity, honesty, and noise.
